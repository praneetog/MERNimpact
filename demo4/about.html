About Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi, born on June 24, 1987, is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward. Widely regarded as one of the greatest players in football history, Messi has a record seven Ballon d'Or awards. Known for his incredible dribbling skills, vision, and goal-scoring ability, Messi has spent the majority of his career with FC Barcelona and later moved to Paris Saint-Germain.
